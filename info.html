About This Project

As someone who recently was searching for jobs and career resources, I found that it is incredibly difficult to know where to turn for the right help. Resume formatting, interview etiquette, and general best-practices when it comes to finding a job are oftentimes not intuitive. Everyone could use help when it comes to the career search. This database is a collection of Bay Area career resources designed to help people get hired and navigate the challenges of landing a good job.
